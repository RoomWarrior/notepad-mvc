Quick Access Toolbar

The Quick Access Toolbar appears to the bottom of the Menubar and provides easy access to the commands you use most frequently (Figure_2). Toolbar is moveable, to customize it drag and drop to certain place.

Commands:

  1. New document
  2. Open
  3. Save
  4. Cut
  5. Copy
  6. Paste
  7. Print
