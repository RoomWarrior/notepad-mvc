Navigating through a Document

As a document gets longer, it gets harder and harder to navigate through it. For example, if you were working on a 1000-line novel, how would you get to the very end of the document or to page 100?.

Scroll bars The scroll bars are the most basic way to move between line in a document. The vertical scroll bar is located along the right side of the window and is used to move up and down in a document.
