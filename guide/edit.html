Cutting

When you cut text, it is removed from its original location and placed in a temporary storage area.

  1. Select the text area that you want to cut.
  2. Click the Cut on the Toolbar (Figure_12) or click Edit and click the Cut(Figure_13) or use keystroke shortcut Ctrl + X .

The text is removed from the document and added to the temp storage.

Copying

When you copy text, the selected text remains in its original location and is also added to the temporary storage.

  1. Select the text area that you want to copy.
  2. Click the Copy on the Toolbar (Figure_14) or click Edit and click the Copy(Figure_15) or use keystroke shortcut Ctrl + C .

The text is copied.

Pasting

After cutting or copying, move the insertion point to a new location in a document and paste.

  1. Click where you want to paste the cut or copied text.
  2. Click the Paste on the Toolbar or click Edit and click the Paste(Figure_16) or use keystroke shortcut Ctrl + V .

The text is pasted.
